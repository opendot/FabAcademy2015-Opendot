The Intelligent Plant's Pot Project (I3P)


What will it do?

The final project will be a Modular and Intelligent Home Plant's Pot based on a new kind of user friendly scalable farming systems. It allows people to grow plants in intelligent pots that have nice and modular design, and which health is continuously monitored in order to grow in optimal conditions.
For more details, check the project page

My project

Therefore, after analyzing what it has been done before and studying what techniques and plant data to monitor, I started designing how my future system will look like and which functionalities to include. So I said that I would like that my project to have:

• Nice and functional design of the POT
• Monitor plant humidity, both on soil and on the environment, and also the temperature of the ambient where the plant grows
• Easy display of sensed information both in the proximity of the plant without the need of computer to be connected, and on the computer via GUI.

From these three objectives, I have thought about the major steps I need to follow in order to develop my project. Therefore, I have identified the following 4 steps:

• Modular Pot design and production: design the pot on the computer, and decide which technique to use in order to build the pot
• Electronic design and assembly: design and build the electronic board that will control the sensors, algorithms that monitor plant health parameters, and sends data to computer
• Sensor installation and programming: design and structure all the wiring schemes of the sensors to the electronic board, then program each sensor to read, display and send data
• GUI: develop a user interface that establishes communication with the electronic hardware, and read data and display it.
