Initial Consideration

after the first five week

From Fixed to Modular

During these first five weeks of FabAcademy i had the opportunity to reflect on my idea of final project, on what to use to build it and on how to do it.
Initially my idea was to create a basket full of backboard, ring and a spring from which detect the intensity applied to the rim, but during these initial weeks i thought it would be better to make a device (composed of 2 parts: one for the input of data and one for the output) that can be applied to the basket.

Thinking in this way i would get many more benefits:

  • The device would be universal and applicable to any type of basket;
    •   
  • Development costs would definitely lower;
    •       
  • The amount of material required for the structural part would be far less;
    •         
  • The device would be easy to apply and transportable.

The device, as mentioned above, would be composed of 2 parts:         

  1. The first part would deal with input data, the force applied to the basket, perhaps using an one axis accelerometer;
    2.         
  2. The second transform incoming data, and via an output device (a display or a series of LEDs) would make visible the value obtained.

The two devices, therefore, should be applied one (the first) directly on the ring of the basket and the other (the second) or on the board or wherever you want to display the value of the dunk.
