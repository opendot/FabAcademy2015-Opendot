Lesson n. 17:

Invention, Intellectual Property, and Income

This week the assignment was about invention, intellectual property and income. The idea behind this exercise was to develop a plan for dissemination of your final project. Basically how do you perceive this project.


Invention & Intellectual properties

The main idea of my project is to make an object attractive to people, able to attract attention and then focus it on the processes used to manufacture, hence precisely the idea of using as much as possible of what I learned during these months.

My will is to leave the project open to all, so as to make it possible for anyone to deal with the process or work use and understand the infinite possibilities that the digital fabrication offers.
