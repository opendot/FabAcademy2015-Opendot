CONCEPT

Rotational casting machine


Everything starts within one of my activities as a maker: Propmaking
A prop (abbreviative for property) is an object used on a movie set. A gun, a mobile phone, a book, a lightsaber, anything that an actor might have to use or touch is considered a prop and it has to be found and colected by the Prop Master or made from scratch by the Prop Maker. In my spare time I run a small propmaking lab with its own small webcomunity called Spaghetti Prop

One thing i usually make, are hollow casts made with silicone mold and cast with polyurethane resin. You can check details about Industrial Rotational Moulding or Casting here. Industrial production uses various types of plastic that melts inside a mold that rotatets for a long time in a owen. What I will be doing is a machine that can work with silicon rubber molds and polyurethane resin for the cast.
The Rotocasting Machine is loaded with closed silicon rubber mold containing a certain amount of premixed two component polyurethane resin and rotates in every direction so that the liquid resin inside keeps moving while it covers the inside walls of the mold. In a lapse of 10 minutes the resin hardens and at the end you have a perfect, durable and lightweight cast.


A silicon rubber mold with a polyurethane cast

I built a prototype two years ago with bicycle part and wood, and I could make a lot of tests on how polyurethane resin reacts inside the mold while spinning at different speeds.

My goal would be to built a Rotocasting Machine that is fully programmable on its spinning speed and periods
