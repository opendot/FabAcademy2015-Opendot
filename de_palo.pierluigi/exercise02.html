Project: Fab Reading Lamp - Computer Modelling -

Soft : Rhinoceros


****************************************************************************************************************



This week I started to use Rhinoceros for modeling 3D. Rhinoceros (abbreviated Rhino) is a commercial 3D computer graphics and computer-aided design (CAD) application software. Rhinoceros for Os X user unfortunaly don’t integrate Grasshopper, a parametric modeling tool.

Well, I have started to work combining samples solids. My first idea was easy: do a reading lamp whit the combination of 3 solids: 2 cones, 1 mirrored to the other one, with a sphere inside.

In reality now I know that it’s a real complex form.The curved lines are more difficult to work that the rectilinear ones.

3 sample solid

After this 1st step I started to try to develop the forms using some Rhino commands. Whit the use of Booleans operations, I was emptied my composite solid.
It’ funny, but it’s not easy ( FOR ME !! ) to be accurate.




In reality I am not very happy of the outcome. The structure is not very elegant. But considering that it is my first exercise, for the moment, I’m settling of this.
