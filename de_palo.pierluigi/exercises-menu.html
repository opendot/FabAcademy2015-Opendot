The Fab Academy 2015 Schedule


In this section I will try to summarize my progress at the Fab Academy, lesson by lesson.

I have called this section ‘Log Book’, using maritime terminology. This is essentially how I would describe my journey at the Fab Academy: as a tumultuous sailing in a deep, endless, fertile sea. As with navigation, there are difficulties that need to be addressed on a daily basis; and like life, satisfaction is a process, not a destination.
