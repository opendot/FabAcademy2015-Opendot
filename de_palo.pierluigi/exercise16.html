---> PART 1 : Modular Machines that Make : Cardboard CNC


The first part of the job has been the assemblage of the prototype in cardboard. For doing it, we have modified the file through Grassopper, suiting it for the thickness of the cardboard in our possession (0,5 mm)





The result is fairly cheap due to the low quality of the cardboard, but the assembled linear stages flowing however correctly



---> PART 2 : Getting Started With Gestalt Nodes


The second part was preparing the Gestalt nodes.

After milling ( lasered here in Open Dot ) the Fabnet I had soldered the components and prepared it for the use.




Finnaly I had soldered the 6 pins cable to connect the Fabnet header on the first node




After soldering the FTDI RS-485 is possible to complete the ghestalt nodes connection :

COMPUTER --> FTDI RS-485 --> FABNET --> 6 PIN CABLE ---> GESTALT --> FLAT 10 PIN CABLE --> GESTALT --> ECT...
