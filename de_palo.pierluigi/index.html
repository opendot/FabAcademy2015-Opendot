Final Project

Photogrammetric 3D Fab Scanner

The idea is to build a 3D scanner that use a traditional photographic camera. The object to scan, will be set on a rotating dish: every time that the dish will rotate of an established angle, the photographic camera will take a picture. With the help of a specific photogrammetric software (MeshLab) it will be possible to create and edit 3D models, that will be subsequently printed.

A feasibility study is in progress
