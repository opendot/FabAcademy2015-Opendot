Final Project


The Photogrammetric 3D Fab Scanner is a closed box witch include: a lighting system, a curved rail for the vertical movement of a consumer digital camera and a rotary dish, where to put down the object to scan. One time that the box closed, the process start: every time that the dish turns by an established angle, the photographic camera will take a picture.

A replicable, low cost, photogrammetry light box.
