The Photogrammetric Fab-Scanner is a cylindrical box, whit 50 cm of diameter.

It's composed from 3 different parts, that work in direct relationship :


- A - The Light Box

- B - The Motorized Stepper Dish

- C - The Motorized Camera Curved Rail




- A - The Light Box
- B - The Motorized Stepper Dish
- C - The Motorized Camera Curved Rail



The object to scan will be set on the rotating dish.

With the lock down of the back doors of the scanner, the process starts:




--> the led lights on, and the photographic camera will take a picture each 15° angle of the rotation support (24 photos).

--> Once the 360°rotation completed, the camera moves in the new positon, and the process "rotation / shooting" start again.

--> The radial movement increases each 45°.

--> The complete process it's composed from 4 differents positions in the camera rail: 0° / +45° / -45° / + 90° (require just 1 photo)



-->Each of it, corresponds to a 360° rotation dish and a set of 24 photos.
