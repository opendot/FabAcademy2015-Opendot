Answering the questions


  • what will it do?

OpenSTAGE is a modular CNC platform basically with 2 axis (X-Y) moved with fishline.

The effector is designed with a multitool snapfit that allows to change easly the tools like pen, laser, mill, hotend etc. The main frame is designed like a tray, and it is possible to use OpenSTAGE on any plane surface.

The control board is also modular and with the basic gantry is possible to mill the 3rd, 4th... axis control board.

It will be possible to control the machine directly from inkscape with a custom plugin, as it will be easy to draw and "print" vector files.

  • who's done what beforehand?

The bigger inspiration comes from Handybot  , also MTM, Risha Laser, Fab-in-a-box.

I will try to make a machine inspired by all the good things coming from them.

  • what parts and systems will be made?

  • what processes will be used?

the chassis is made by a lasercut

It is possible to made the effector by cnc, 3d printer or molding

carriages, idlers and various supports ar made with a 3d printer but is possible to make them also with a laser cutter

the electronic board is fabbable with a fiber laser, cnc milling or classical etching
