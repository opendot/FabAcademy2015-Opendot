Molding and casting

Assignment

  • design a 3D mold, machine it, and cast parts from it

Snap-it

Two years ago I attended 3D print Exhibition contest during Maker Faire Rome and I win the first prize with a little sueful object

this is the LINK of the contest

For casting and molding module, I decided to use this little 3d printed object to make a mold. Ususally I use the pink silicon to create the mold, But my friend Simone Boasso told me about by mixing silicone and cornstarch is possible to make a paste like Sugru and i would to try!

Classical pink silicon is selled in 2 components to mix in equal parts. To make he new magic paste I started mixing 50/50 and adding cornstarch as the paste doesn't glued on my hand

cornstarch/silicon - classical pink silicon

I filled the mold with the same quantity of simple bi-component resin to see the difference .

Of course the pink silicon produce more detailed pieces, but the Magic potion is perfect to make some mold in case we havent the appropriate silicon! .

classical pink silicon - cornstarch/silicon


Milling a wax block

To do the exercise with CNC mill I design a custom puppet of ironman in Rhino using 2 STLs files from thingiverse, than I used boolean operation to create a mold


DOWNLOAD ironman mold
then I milled it with a MODELA MDX 40
Modela Player is a simple program that help to mill easly, after import the stl file is possible to select the tip for rough and finishing.
In my case I used the same tip for both. one round 3mm tip.
The prough process take many times but it was the first time tht I used this machine and in that time I was alone in the lab so I prefered to work in safe conditions :)





After fixing the wax block on the working area with tape I do the zero position for all axes and starting the job



This is the result, unfortunately the power go down and I had to do again the zero position but I can't chose the same point.

the next step was to fill the mold with silicon
 



Next I filled the silicon with resin
